Questions

The honest answers.

Who is this for?

Operators who already know what good work looks like and don't want to manage a vendor to get it. Agencies clearing backlog. Nonprofit EDs swimming in board prep. Founders running ahead of their team.

What counts as one task?

One discrete deliverable, bounded scope. If your ask is bigger we flag it before starting and break it into two — no surprise invoices.

How does payment work?

Card on file via Stripe. Charged on task approval. No subscription, no monthly minimum, no auto-renewal.

Why "Groundwork"?

It's the layer underneath. The thing that lets the visible work happen.

What if I need something out of scope?

Tell us anyway. Custom builds and integrations get scoped separately. Sometimes a few flat-rate tasks are the right answer; sometimes we'll point you to someone better suited.

What is "Stack it"?

The other mode. Instead of sending us a task, we recommend the right tools for the function and set you up to handle it yourself. Tool links earn affiliate commissions — that's how Stack it stays free.
